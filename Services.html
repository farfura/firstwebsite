Primary Offerings

Potential Offerings

Once the gap is targeted, Organica will focus on introducing a full organic makeup line. Its sole purpose will be to cater every skin tone and type (sensitive skin, dry skin, oily skin etc.) .As majority of makeup line does not include spf which is essential for protection from harmful sun rays, all of our makeup products will contain spf (sun protection factor) 50.
